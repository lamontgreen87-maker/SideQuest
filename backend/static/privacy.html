Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-09

This Privacy Policy explains how SideQuest AI (the "Service") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the app or related services.

Information We Collect

  • Account details: email address (if you sign in by email) or wallet address (if you sign in with a wallet).
  • Gameplay data: characters, sessions, messages, and settings you create in the app.
  • Payment data: transaction hashes, amounts, and the wallet address used to purchase credits.
  • Technical data: IP address, device identifiers, app version, and diagnostic logs needed to operate and debug the Service.

How We Use Information

  • Operate the Service, including creating sessions and generating responses.
  • Process purchases and credit balances tied to your account or wallet.
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and diagnose issues.
  • Improve features and performance.

AI Processing

Your gameplay inputs and related session data are processed by AI models to generate responses. This processing may occur on our servers or a provider we use to deliver the Service.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers who help us operate the Service (hosting, analytics, payment processing) and only as needed.

Retention

We retain data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. You can request deletion of your data.

Your Choices

  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data.
  • You can stop using the Service at any time.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect data, but no system is 100% secure.

Children

The Service is not intended for children under 13. Do not use the Service if you are under 13.
